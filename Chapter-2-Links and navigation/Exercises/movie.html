    Synopsis

    After fleeing from a stage show, the illusionists known as the Four Horsemen find themselves in more trouble in Macau, China. A devious tech wizard forces the infamous magicians to steal a powerful chip that can control all of the world's computers. Meanwhile, a vengeful FBI agent hatches his own plot against the infamous perpetrator, the man he blames for the death of his father.
