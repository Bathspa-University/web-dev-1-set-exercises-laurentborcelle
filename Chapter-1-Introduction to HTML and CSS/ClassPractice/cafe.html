Iced Caramel Macchiato

The Iced Caramel Macchiato is one of the most popular espresso-based drinks.

Skip the queue at the coffee shop and make your own iced caramel macchiato at home! Easy to make and ready to serve in minutes, this is the perfect hot weather pick me up.

An iced macchiato is made very similar to an iced latte, but the latte can be made with strong brewed coffee if you don’t have espresso powder or instant coffee. In this I haven’t really enjoyed the taste of cold brew or strong brewed hot coffee, but you could always try it if you don’t have anything else. The nice thing about instant coffee powder is that it dissolves instantly so you can make it without turning on any appliance. If you don’t have the caramel syrup, you can make that at home with a small saucepan, sugar, and water.

Directions

Step 1. Dissolve the instant coffee or espresso powder in 6 tbsp of water.

Step 2. Add caramel syrup to the bottom of a glass. Start with 1 to 1.5 tbsp of the caramel syrup. You can add more to taste at the end.

Step 3. Then add milk. Once you have the milk in the cup, bring it to almost the top with ice. Pour the dissolved coffee over the top.

Step 4. Drizzle with caramel topping. Mix and taste, adjust sweetness if needed.

Ingredients:

Vanilla Syrup
Milk
Espresso
Caramel Syrup
